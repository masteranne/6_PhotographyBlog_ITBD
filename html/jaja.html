Jaja Samaniego

Biography


Part of growing up is her fondness for film, fashion magazines and MTV. She love looking at how colors, textures, shapes and other elements combine together to create meaningful images. As a visual person, She wanted to create a concrete image of her ideas. She tried drawing and painting but unfortunately she wasn't good at it. The idea of photography came to Her when she wanted my drawings to look as realistic as possible. Back in college, she had my very first black-and-white photography class. From then on, she was hooked. Jaja started borrowing my friend's camera every time she go home to my province just so she can take pictures of the locals. Her Dad finally got me my first dLSR and she just wanted to make the most of it. So that was the start.
