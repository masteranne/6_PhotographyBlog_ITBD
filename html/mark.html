Mark Nicdao



Biography


He became one of the pioneers to embrace digital photography in the Philippines after launching his career as a professional photographer in 2003. His shots of various local and Hollywood celebrities have been on the cover of different local and international magazines and campaigns. Mark, who described himself as a modern-day philanthropist, has also collaborated with organizations such as the UN and UNICEF in supporting and promoting their advocacies.To most showbiz fans, photographer Mark Nicdao is best known for capturing the fashionable, candid, and glamorous sides of celebrities for magazine covers, fashion billboards, and fashion editorials. But last January 26, the celebrity photographer unveiled another facet of his photography skills when he worked with luxury car brand Maserati for a rare photo exhibit at The Wellington Centre in Bonifacio Global City. Maserati named Mark as one of their "People of Passion" this 2018, and the brand collaborated with the photographer to create his photo exhibit, "X-Ray."
