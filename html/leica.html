LEICA Q2 MONOCHROM

Camera

Embrace the soul of photography

The Leica Q2 Monochrom produces brilliant, exceptionally sharp images. This is due to the new specialized sensor developed exclusively for this camera. Unlike its chromatic counterparts, the monochrome sensor features no color filter array – with no interpolation required, only the pure light levels are captured and rendered sharper








Outstanding performance in any light

The built-in Summilux 28 f/1.7 ASPH. prime lens is distinguished by both the razor-sharp details and exceptionally soft bokeh it renders. As the fastest lens in its segment, it is perfectly suited to the sensor of the Leica Q2 Monochrom, and delivers outstanding results even in the darkest and most difficult light.









Always in control

To ensure photographers have full control over their compositions, the Leica Q2 Monochrom sports an OLED viewfinder with a resolution of 3.68 megapixels. See each and every bit of depth even within the darkest of black levels to ensure the photo is composed exactly as desired.









Brave any weather

Harsh environmental conditions and even the risk of rain will not faze this versatile camera. Equipped with dust and splash protection (rated IP52*), the Leica Q2 Monochrom is always ready to capture that perfect interplay of light and shadow even through equally dramatic weather.

Wide ISO Range- Show off your skills in the dark with awide ISO range of 100 – 25,600* (expandable to 51,200) to deliver sharper images with less noiseat higher shutter speeds even when handheld.










LEICA SL2-S

Camera

Two worlds. One choice.

Leica SL2-S enables photographers and videographers to achieve the legendary Leica look in a fast and capable camera without compromise. Combining a new 24MP CMOS-BSI sensor, the latest Maestro III processor, and two separate shooting modes for photo and video, the Leica SL2-S offers an unparalleled, intuitive hybrid photography experience and the best of two worlds.








Design Made to Inspire

The design of the Leica SL2-S recalls the iconic Leica identity and traditional design DNA elements that the brand has become renowned and loved for. The best ergonomics, highest quality materials and state of the art technology are combined into a camera that is made to perform and built to last.









Control and handling

A unique user interface, separated by photo and video functions, simple and intuitive to allow the photographer to focus on picture taking – developed through feedbacks of customers and professional photographers/videographers worldwide.









Cine Mode

The Leica SL2-S captures 4K and Cine4K 10-bit 4:2:2 videos with Leica’s L-Log gamma, at frame rates of up to 60 fps. Thanks to the integrated viewing LUTs, the user is able to have full control throughout the recording. Another pro-level feature is the camera’s unlimited video recording time, which means that the video length is only limited by the SD card or external memory capacity.









LEICA M10-R

Camera

Redefining image quality.

No other M-Camera is geared towards maximum image quality in such an uncompromising manner like the Leica M10-R. Thus the camera fully realizes the capabilities of the prime M-Lenses and offers new stylistic and compositional approaches to the photographer.








Increased resolution

With a resolution of over 40 megapixels, the Leica M10-R’s newly developed image sensor is able to capture even the finest textures and details. Its base sensitivity of ISO 100, combined with fast Leica M-Lenses, makes it easy and flexible for shooting wide open and achieving maximum background blur and strong bokeh even in bright daytime light.









Special components for an exceptional performance

The super quiet mechanical shutter, first established with the Leica M10-P, allows for stealthy operation and minimal vibrations to minimize camera shake and help ensure sharper photos.









Hand assembly for outstanding craftsmanship

The Leica M10-R is constructed by highly trained specialists, who assemble the elaborately engineered components into a product that is much more than the sum of its parts. The rangefinder alone is comprised of over 100 individual parts, the majority of which move together in harmony with every turn of the lens.

APO-SUMMICRON-M 35 f/2 ASPH

Lens

For pictures that make history.

The Summilux-M 21mm f/1.4 ASPH. consists of ten lenses in eight groups and incorporates five separate lenses with anomalous partial dispersion. As a result, vignetting and distortion are both excellently corrected. The floating element retains the lenses’ high resolution, even in the close-up range. A patented, rectangular lens hood fits Series VIII filters.
